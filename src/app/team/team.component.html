Meet Our Team

At the heart of Waypoint are those who serve. Here are a few of those who have dedicated their lives to this mission.

Chris Morse

Executive Director

Chris lives with his wife, Pam, and son, Declan, in Forward Township, PA. He has been serving as the worship leader and the Creative Arts Director at House of Faith in West Newton, PA for the past 17 years, and was just recently ordained as an Associate Pastor there. He worked as a carpenter for 25 years until God called him into full-time ministry. He is also the Director of Worship for the Coalition of Fellowship. Chris heads up Waypoint Worship, has a mentoring group of 21 young adults, and works day-to-day at the youth center.

Pam Morse

President

Pam lives in Forward Township, PA with her husband, Chris, and son, Declan. Pam works full time at PricewaterhouseCoopers as a Director in the Wealth Management Tax Services group. She has dedicated all of her free time to ministry, the youth center, and is a mentor alongside her husband to 21 young adults.

Brigid Kolenc

Executive Coordinator

Brigid lives in Forward Township, PA with her husband, Dean. She works part-time as a custom watercolor illustrator and a volunteer coordinator at the youth center. Brigid is a vocalist as well as the Social Media Director of Waypoint Worship, and is a member of the worship team at House of Faith, which has been her church for 21 years.

Jared Roll

Secretary

Jared lives in West Newton, PA, just down the road from the youth center. Jared's heart is serving the young, whether in the Dominican Republic, or just in town. He works full-time, and spends his free time as a youth leader for Bible Alliance Church.
