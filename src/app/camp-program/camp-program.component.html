Camp Program

About

Waypoint's 2020 summer camp program is a fun and exciting way to instill hope into the surrounding youth. Due to COVID-19 and the circumstances that came with it, the students were robbed of their summer. Many kids missed out on opportunities to play the sport they usually would during this time. Our camps will fix that problem. Our mission is to mentor and train the students to build their confidence and skills through our 4 day camp. The camps will take place Monday through Thursday from 9 AM to 1PM, and the days will include a lunch along with exhibitions, drills, conditioning, and other activities to push and develop their skills.

Our wide variety of camps include:
  • Football
  • Cheer
  • Softball/Baseball
  • Art
  • Basketball
  • Outdoors
  • Soccer
  • Swimming
  • Drama

Our staff, volunteers, and local professionals are ready to build into the youth and give back to the community. We believe in the kids, and these camps are one way for us to show it to them. We are so excited for Waypoint's 2020 summer camp program. Cannot wait to see you there!
